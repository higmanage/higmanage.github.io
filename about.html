Who are We?

Hillstone Investments Group LLC (HIG) is a fully integrated real estate development and investment company based in San Jose, California. Our focus is on creating and preserving value for our equity investors through the use of our specialized knowledge of the real-estate development in Silicon Valley. HIG was formed in 2019 by Dylan Nguyen, Thanh Nguyen and David Zhang to acquire, renovate and sell single family house in the Bay Area. Recognizing opportunities in creating extra value, HIG has expanded our business into ground-up development of multi-family real estate with our experience in the re-entitlement process.
